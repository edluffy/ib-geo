# Homogenisation of Urban Landscapes
## Key terms
> Homogenous - of the same or similar nature.
> In Urban Geography - the process in which different landscapes in a country increasingly resemble those found in a different country.
> Heterogeneous - The nature of oppositoin, or contrast of qualities.

## TNCs
